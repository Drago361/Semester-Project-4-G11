Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a book?

You can search for books by entering a title, author, or keyword in the search bar on our homepage. You can also click on one of our suggested categories to browse books by genre.

Is BookFind free to use?

Yes, BookFind is completely free to use. We provide book recommendations and information to help you discover your next great read without any charge.

Can I purchase books directly from BookFind?

BookFind is a book discovery platform, not a bookstore. We provide information about books and link to reputable retailers where you can purchase the books that interest you.

How can I save my favorite books?

Once you've created an account, you can save books to your favorites by clicking the heart icon on any book card. You can access your saved books by visiting your profile page.

Does BookFind have mobile apps?

Currently, BookFind is available as a mobile-responsive website that works well on all devices. We are developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, which will be available soon.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by using the contact form on our Contact page. Alternatively, you can email us directly at support@bookfind.com.

How often is the book database updated?

We update our book database daily to ensure you have access to the latest releases and most accurate information. New books are typically added within 24-48 hours of their publication.
